Why CHYK

What You Gain

📚

Vedantic Wisdom

Access structured study of Upanishads, Bhagavad Gita, and Swami Chinmayananda's teachings through weekly classes and residential camps.

🌍

Global Community

Connect with 50,000+ like-minded young seekers across India and 12 countries. Find your tribe, find lifelong friendships.

🏔️

Transformative Experiences

From Himalayan yatras to national conventions — CHYK experiences are designed to push boundaries and expand your sense of self.

🎭

Arts & Culture

Express yourself through CHYK's rich cultural ecosystem — theatre, music, art, and the beloved Udghosh National Youth Festival.

🤝

Seva & Service

Make a real difference through YEP, national outreach projects, and community service initiatives that go beyond individual benefit.

🎯

Leadership Development

Lead activities, organise events, guide younger members — CHYK is where you grow as a human being and as a leader.

Common Questions

Membership itself is free. Individual programmes, camps, and events may have participation fees that vary by centre and activity.

Pre-CHYK is for ages 14–17. CHYK is for ages 18–28. Some centres may have flexibility — check with your local centre.

CHYK welcomes young seekers from all backgrounds. Vedanta is a universal philosophy — its wisdom belongs to all of humanity.

CHYK has an active online community and remote learning programmes. You can connect with us online and attend virtual events while we work to establish a centre near you.
