How Wings Work

  • ✦ All CHYK members can join any wing
  • ✦ Each wing has its own coordinator and projects
  • ✦ Wings meet independently and at CHYK events
  • ✦ Wing work counts toward CHYK seva hours
  • ✦ Some wings have national and zonal chapters
