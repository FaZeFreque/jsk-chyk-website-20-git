Udghosh

Udghosh is written entirely by CHYK members. If you have a reflection, story, poem, or essay you'd like to share with the CHYK world, we'd love to feature your voice.

Submit a Piece Read Articles

Submission Guidelines

  • ✦ 500–2000 words for essays and reflections
  • ✦ Original, unpublished work only
  • ✦ All topics relating to Vedanta, CHYK life, culture
  • ✦ Poetry and short fiction welcome
  • ✦ Submissions reviewed by editorial team

Publication Frequency

Udghosh is published annually. The editorial team reviews submissions on a rolling basis throughout the year.
