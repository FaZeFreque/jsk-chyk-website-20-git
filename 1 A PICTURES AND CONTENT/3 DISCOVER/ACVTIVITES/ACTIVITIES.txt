1. Study


Weekly Chyk Classes
No syllabus. No exam. Just a circle of curious minds, an ancient text, and a question that refuses to stay quiet. Every week, CHYKs across India — in living rooms, temple courtyards, and college corners — sit down together to do something radically simple: think. Study. Grow. These 90-minute circles have been quietly changing the way young people see themselves and the world around them.
